Tonkotsu Ramen

Welcome to my Japanese recipe blog! Today, Iâ€™ll guide you through an authentic **Tonkotsu Ramen** recipe, a rich and flavorful dish that originated in Fukuoka, Japan.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork bones
  • 6 cups water
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • Ramen noodles
  • Boiled egg
  • Nori (seaweed)
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil the pork bones for 10 minutes to remove impurities.
  2. In a large pot, add water, garlic, and onion. Simmer with the pork bones for 4 hours.
  3. After 4 hours, strain the broth, add soy sauce, and miso paste.
  4. Boil the ramen noodles according to package instructions.
  5. In a bowl, add noodles, pour broth, and top with boiled egg, nori, and green onions.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade Tonkotsu Ramen!

Chef's Tips

  • For a creamier broth, add a small amount of heavy cream.
  • Experiment with different toppings like bamboo shoots or bean sprouts.
  • Use fresh noodles for the best texture.
  • Adjust the seasoning to your taste.
